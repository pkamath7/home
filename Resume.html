Resume

SUMMARY

A software engineer with five years of experience in developing algorithms, embedded frameworks, and end to end production quality software solutions for infotainment applications with a focus on audio technology. My research interests include signal processing, machine learning, audio and speech processing.

EDUCATION

University of California San Diego Expected June 2023

completed courses: Filter banks and Wavelets, Principles of AI: probabilistic reason and learning, Digital Image Processing

BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE), Bengaluru, India June 2016

Bachelor of Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering GPA: 9.66/10

TECHNICAL SKILLS

Core competencies: Digital Signal Processing, Embedded System Software
Languages: C, C++, C#, Python, SHARC assembly
Software: MATLAB, TensorFlow, Pytorch, CCES, Audacity, Visual Studio, Git, SVN

WORK EXPERIENCE

Analog Devices Inc (ADI), Bengaluru, India Aug 2016- Aug 2021

Software Engineer, Infotainment Processing and Connectivity group

  • Developed signal processing libraries and toolboxes and robust systems solutions for audio applications such as Active Noise Cancellation, Echo Cancellation, Noise reduction. Evaluated solutions in a test car and tuned their performance.
  • Designed and delivered an optimal multichannel feedback canceller for in-car entertainment (reduced resource consumption by ~40% enabling its deployment on low-cost DSPs)
  • Awarded Analog Devices Ahead of What's Possible Award 2020 for contributions to development of software and audio algorithms as a part of the team that developed ADI’s first 1GHZ single core DSP.
  • Designed and developed HIS-MISRA complaint software, framework, and drivers to enable applications on SHARC family of processors.
  • Implemented front end (windows form and WPF) and backend software features(.NET based) for Sigma Studio- a graphical programming tool enabling seamless audio application creation, deployment and tuning for ADI DSPs
  • Performed the duties of configuration controller and helped achieve AUTOSPICE Level 3 certification for the team
  • Lead the CSR project for prototyping a smart wearable device to introduce play and learn for visually impaired children

Analog Devices Inc (ADI), Bengaluru, India Jan 2016- Jul 2016

Software Engineering Intern, Infotainment Processing and Connectivity group

  • Developed, optimized, and ported signal processing algorithms for ADI floating and fixed-point DSPs (SHARC family and ADAU145x processors) to enable quick signal chain development and evaluation for customers

ACADEMIC PROJECTS

Design and implementation of LSTF dynamic scheduler for RTOS Nov 2015

  • Designed and implemented the LSTF scheduling algorithm in C++ for FreeRTOS for TIVA TM4C123GXL
  • Benchmarked the same against existing deadline based dynamic priority pre-emptive scheduling schemes

Traffic sign detection using template matching and Dynamic Time Warping Apr 2015

  • Designed a traffic sign detection algorithm based on template matching and Dynamic Time warping to detect stop signs for advanced driver assistance systems using MATLAB.

PUBLICATIONS AND PRESENTATIONS

  • Published a paper on “Howling suppressor for in car communication (ICC) applications” at Analog Devices General Technical conference (GTC) 2020 and demonstrated the same in GTC 2021.
  • Awarded the best demo for the presentation of “Acoustic Echo Cancellation in in-vehicle communication” at the Analog Devices India Technical Conference 2019.
  • “Design and Implementation of LST Based Dynamic Scheduler on Real Time OS”, International Journal of Electrical Electronics & Computer Science Engineering Special Issue - NEWS 2016
